# 🎓 Student Dropout Risk Analysis Using Power BI

## 📌 Project Objective
Identify risk factors contributing to student dropout using KPIs and visuals built in Power BI. This project focuses on attendance, participation, study hours, and academic performance.

---

## 📊 Dataset Summary
- **Total Records:** 30 students
- **Columns:**
  - `Student_ID`: Unique ID
  - `Study_Hours_per_Week`: Time spent on studies weekly
  - `Attendance_%`: Class attendance rate
  - `Avg_Marks`: Average marks in exams
  - `Distractions`: Distraction level (High / Medium / Low)
  - `Final_Result`: Pass / Fail

---

## 🔧 Tools Used
- **Excel** – Data cleaning & calculation
- **Power BI** – KPIs, Bar, Pie, and Interactive visuals
- **Canva** – Infographic presentation
- **Notion** – Case study documentation

---

## 📌 Key KPIs
- **Total Students:** 20  
- **Dropout Rate:** 30%

---

## 📈 Key Visuals
- Attendance impact chart
- Participation pie chart
- Marks by gender
- KPI cards
- Gender slicer

---

## 💡 Insights
- 🔴 Students with <60% attendance = 100% failure  
- 📉 Low participation leads to higher dropout risk  
- 📘 Students studying >10 hrs/week scored above 75%  
- 🚫 High distraction level correlates with failure

---

## 🎯 Recommendations
- Monitor students with attendance <70%  
- Early mentorship & student engagement  
- Track weekly study hours and provide resources

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Power BI dashboards enable institutions to visualize patterns and proactively support students at risk of dropping out.
